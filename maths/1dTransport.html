1D Transport Equation

A wave moves. A signal echoes. Information flows without distortion.

The Governing Principle

∂u/∂t + c ∂u/∂x = 0

This isn't just an equation. It's the signature of pure motion.

Meaning in Motion

u(x, t) = f(x − c t)

Any initial pattern f(x) moves rigidly at speed c. No decay. No distortion.

The Language of Characteristics

dx/dt = c,   du/dt = 0

Each point is a passenger on a straight line: a trajectory of pure inheritance.

Initial Storytelling

u(x, 0) = φ(x) ⟹ u(x, t) = φ(x − c t)

The initial pulse φ(x) is merely translated. It remembers everything.

Numerical Ritual (Upwind Discretization)

uᵢⁿ⁺¹ = uᵢⁿ − (c Δt/Δx)(uᵢⁿ − uᵢ₋₁ⁿ)

If c > 0, take information from behind. If c < 0, from ahead.

Why It Matters

  • First step to understanding fluid flow and hyperbolic PDEs
  • Governs convection in CFD simulations
  • Appears in electromagnetics, traffic, weather, even finance
  • No entropy generated – this is the cleanest motion possible

The Conservation View

∂u/∂t + ∂(c u)/∂x = 0

It’s more than motion. It’s conservation of quantity u flowing at speed c.

When You Break It (Add Viscosity)

∂u/∂t + c ∂u/∂x = ν ∂²u/∂x²

Now you get diffusion—smoothing, irreversibility, loss of memory.

In a Nutshell

The 1D transport equation is not about what changes—it's about what **doesn't**.
