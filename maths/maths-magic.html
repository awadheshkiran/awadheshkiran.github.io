9 x 1 = 9

9 x 7 = 63

You can see that the first half of the digits in the answer is one less than the number which it is multiplied ,right? And together they sum upto 9.
Closely examining 9 x 6 = 54.
6 - 1 = 5 _ first digit
5 + 4 = 9 _ second number

Now, replicate your idea to a two digit number,say 43.
